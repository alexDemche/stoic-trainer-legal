Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. DATA WE COLLECT

To ensure the app functions correctly and to personalize your experience, we may collect the following data:

  • Name or Nickname: To address you within the app.
  • Date of Birth (Optional): Used strictly for the "Memento Mori" feature (life weeks visualization). You can provide this at your discretion within the app.
  • Game Progress: Level, rank, wisdom points, practice statistics.
  • Authentication Data (Google/Apple/Telegram): If you choose to log in via Google, Apple, or Telegram, we receive your basic account data (such as email, name, and a unique identifier) to securely back up your progress and enable cross-device synchronization.
  • Technical Data: Device ID for account synchronization.
  • Third-Party Links: The app contains links to external resources. We do not collect data on your clicks, but external sites may use their own cookies.
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: To discover, connect, and sync data with your "Stoic Stone" hardware device.
  • Location Access: On certain Android versions, location access is technically required to perform Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scanning. We use this strictly for device discovery. We do not track, store, or share your physical location.
  • Analytics & Diagnostics: We use third-party services (like Google Firebase) to collect anonymous diagnostic data (crash logs, device model, OS version) and app usage. These services process your IP address to determine approximate coarse location (city/country level). We do not track or store your exact physical coordinates.

2. HOW WE USE DATA

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your profile and statistics.
  • Synchronizing progress between devices.
  • Improving app stability, monitoring performance, and fixing crashes via anonymous diagnostic reports.

3. STORAGE & SECURITY

We use secure servers to store your progress. We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties.

We may share anonymous, non-identifiable technical data with trusted third-party analytics providers (like Google Firebase) solely to maintain and improve the app.

4. ACCOUNT DELETION

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the profile settings.

Path: Profile -> Delete All Data & Profile.

If you cannot access the app, you can request data deletion by emailing us at stoic.labs.dev@gmail.com.

This action is irreversible.
